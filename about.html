Professional Background

My Journey & Mission

I work at the intersection of project management, data, and people, helping organizations do more meaningful work, more accountably. My background in Computer Science (Beirut Arab University, CGPA 3.8) gives me a strong analytical foundation that I bring into everything—whether it's designing indicator tracking sheets, running post-distribution monitoring, or producing evidence-based reports for donors.

Most recently, I completed the MEAL for NGOs certification from the AUB NGO Initiative (NGOi) with a perfect score of 100% across all assessments. This formalized my field experience and added a rigorous methodology that I apply to coordinate project activities, ensure data-driven decisions, and strengthen accountability mechanisms.

Bridges, Not Barriers

I believe that university majors shouldn't limit you. My Computer Science degree is the tool that helps me serve my passion for humanitarian operations and community impact in a stronger, smarter, and more meaningful way.
